IRA: Inspiring Resilience & Awareness
*A Women-Centric Health Empowerment Initiative*

IRA is a web-based initiative that aims to empower women through the dissemination of information regarding physical health, mental well-being, hygiene, and preventive care. It provides a platform that is safe and accessible for women to learn, connect with, share, and grow in confidence.

---

Vision

To create a world where every woman has access to the factual health information, emotional support, and a community that empowers her to take charge of her well-being.
